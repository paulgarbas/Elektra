Our services

World-quality products deserve superb technical back up, and that is what we aim to provide. All of our product ranges are backed by the highly trained services of an electrical engineer and a professional team of technicians, specifically trained to provide:

Support

  • Over-the-phone technical assistance
  • Trouble shooting and remedial action
  • After sales services
  • Free Delivery

Custom design

  • Lighting survey / site visit
  • Lighting layout design
  • Light intensity calculations – DIALux
  • Custom designed engineering solutions

Inspection & Training

  • On-site inspection where necessary, especially in large projects where the customer has invested on branded quality light fittings
  • Pre-installation training, to ensure correct installation of the fittings

Maintenance

  • Preventative and corrective maintenance
  • Maintenance agreements, tailored to the needs of individual clients, contribute to the reliability of this service, providing constant peace of mind
